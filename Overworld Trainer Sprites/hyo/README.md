
# hyo's reworked FRLG-styled RSE overworld sprites

**If used or edited, please give credit to hyo.**

All of the sprites were made from the groundup to match the style of FireRed and Leafgreen. Please be aware that this collection is different from the one's made by Poffin_Case!

### NOTES (IMPORTANT!)
- The player character (Brendan, May, Red, Gold) folders also include .pal files for both their palette and reflection palette. **PLEASE** make sure to place them in the 'graphics/object_events/palettes' folder.
- The player character (Brendan, May, Red, Gold) folders also include a 'character_icon' image. **PLEASE** make sure to place them in the 'graphics/pokenav/region_map folder'.
